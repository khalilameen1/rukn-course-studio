# 02 RESEARCH MARKET AND VOICE CALIBRATION

## Separate evidence roles

Every source receives one or more explicit roles
Never let a source acquire authority merely because it is long polished or similar in tone

### FACT AUTHORITY

Used for factual technical legal theological medical financial platform or product claims

Prefer in order when applicable

1. Primary texts standards laws and official documentation
2. Original research datasets and first party records
3. Recognised professional or academic references
4. Current experienced practitioner evidence
5. High quality secondary explanation

### MARKET EVIDENCE

Used to understand actual offers constraints language workflows expectations and buyer behaviour in the target market
Use current local examples and multiple signals
Do not infer performance from mere public existence
Do not treat the loudest local style as the only valid local style

### PRACTITIONER REALITY

Used for actual starting states attempts failures corrections tradeoffs performance friction variation access counterpart behaviour completion handoff and what happens after a clean explanation ends
This role is mandatory whenever the promise includes practical performance interaction teaching diagnosis operation creation or real application

Prefer

- Detailed postmortems and case retrospectives
- Public or authorised practitioner discussions in professional groups forums and communities
- Question and answer threads where context attempt symptom diagnosis and correction are visible
- Issue trackers support threads implementation notes and debugging discussions
- Long form interviews workflow breakdowns field notes and work diaries
- Experienced practitioner comparisons that state conditions and tradeoffs

Do not let promotional tool copy polished portfolio commentary or an ideal workflow description substitute for practitioner reality

### VOICE REFERENCE

Used only to calibrate human rhythm explanation density sentence construction transition behaviour and domain native speech
It is not factual authority unless independently qualified

### FLOW REFERENCE

Used to study how a real instructor carries a long explanation
It may be an imperfect transcript
Never copy its wording catchphrases errors or episode structure
Never import its presenter persona reaction style artificial suspense editing rhythm or entertainment mannerisms

### EXERCISE OR CASE MATERIAL

Used as raw material for a learning task
Track rights privacy and whether the case is real synthetic or adapted

## Source firewall

Maintain these separations

- Facts come from fact authority
- Local assumptions come from market evidence
- Voice comes from voice references
- Teaching sequence comes from the capability graph
- Course promise comes from the intake and thesis
- Examples must be verified or clearly synthetic

A transcript cannot establish a technical fact because the speaker sounds credible
Official documentation cannot dictate natural spoken delivery
Social posts cannot define the full curriculum
Vendor marketing cannot define realistic capability or limitations

## Practitioner reality research contract

Build `PRACTITIONER_REALITY_PACK` before mapping a substantial practical or applied course
Official and recognised sources explain what should be true
Practitioner reality evidence exposes how the performance actually begins varies breaks gets corrected and reaches completion
Both are required when the promise includes execution interaction perception teaching judgement diagnosis or application

Collect evidence across the full real performance episode rather than only the central technique or concept
Look for

- Incomplete inputs unclear goals ambiguous language noisy signals or missing context
- Access permission environment setup tool material or counterpart problems
- First attempts that looked reasonable but failed
- Symptoms practitioners use to locate the real cause
- Corrections that worked and corrections that created another problem
- Time cost scope quality and client tradeoffs
- Feedback misunderstanding correction approval escalation completion and handoff friction
- Edge cases learner customer counterpart tool material environment and local market constraints
- Workarounds that are acceptable temporarily and boundaries where they become bad practice

## Capability adapter evidence contract

Research must validate not only what is true but how the promised capability is actually performed learned corrected and represented
Build evidence per capability adapter rather than assuming that a generic course family supplies the learning loop

For every row in `CAPABILITY_ADAPTER_MAP` establish

- Authentic inputs signals counterpart behaviour or environment
- The decisions and actions that distinguish capable from superficial performance
- Common novice substitutions and why they fail
- Observable error or quality signals
- Corrections retries and changed conditions practitioners actually use
- The point at which performance becomes independent
- The smallest attachment evidence that represents the capability honestly
- Any source authority safety privacy tool language or market overlay

A reusable adapter supplies a candidate pattern
Course specific evidence must still verify how that pattern appears in the target field audience and context

A custom adapter moves through these states

```yaml
custom_adapter_evidence:
  custom_adapter_id:
  status: PROVISIONAL
  capability_ids: []
  practitioner_performance_sources: []
  authority_sources: []
  authentic_inputs_and_variations: []
  decision_and_action_loop: []
  failure_signals: []
  correction_retry_and_transfer_patterns: []
  independence_evidence:
  honest_project_evidence:
  unresolved_conflicts: []
  acceptance_reason:
```

Set status to `ACCEPTED` only when the performance loop is supported across the material conditions that matter to the promise
Set status to `REJECTED` when the loop is generic inferred from topic headings or contradicted by stronger evidence
Do not map modules while a core custom adapter remains provisional

For work language research the actual role interaction and language evidence together
Collect turn functions incoming language likely misunderstandings register pronunciation or listening demands branching responses documentation and handoff reality
Do not build the course from vocabulary lists grammar catalogues or translated scripts alone

For sales service support or call handling research listening diagnosis branch decisions emotional and objection signals tool or case states follow up escalation closure and documentation
For teaching research learner misconceptions response evidence explanation attempts correction choices second attempts and transfer checks
For production technical analytical knowledge and physical capabilities research the native work samples failure signatures correction patterns and completion standards of those modes

Do not copy practitioner scripts threads or identifiable cases
Synthesize verified patterns into original Rukn teaching situations and exercises

## Community evidence protocol

A forum reply group post comment or anecdote is not automatically factual authority
Use it to reveal a plausible real condition performance step interaction signal failure mode or decision boundary then verify technical factual legal safety or platform claims through the appropriate authority

Prefer a contribution when it includes

- Enough context to know what task market tool or constraint is being discussed
- A described attempt rather than a slogan
- An observable symptom result or consequence
- Reasoning that connects the symptom to a cause
- A correction test or later outcome
- Limits exceptions or disagreement from other experienced people

Reject or lower confidence when evidence is promotional anonymous without context copied hearsay engagement bait or a universal claim built from one success
Do not rank advice by likes confidence tone or follower count

For a material practitioner pattern seek independent corroboration across different people cases or source types
Record meaningful disagreement rather than averaging incompatible contexts
One exceptionally detailed primary practitioner case may establish a case example but not a universal prevalence claim

Use private groups closed communities or supplied conversations only when access and reuse are authorised
Remove personal identifiers confidential client details credentials private files and unnecessary quotations
Do not expose source participants in the learner facing course

## Voice calibration depth

When a suitable reference creator exists and sufficient material is available review at least 20000 words before extracting a voice profile

A suitable main reference has

- Abundant authentic material
- Sustained explanation rather than isolated captions
- A language variety compatible with the target
- Credible domain fluency or a transferable explanatory style
- Enough variation to distinguish natural habits from one episode choices

If 20000 usable words are unavailable do not pretend to have deep calibration
Use multiple secondary references or a neutral natural practitioner voice

Extract abstract properties only

- Average thought length
- How context is introduced
- How examples enter
- How terms are explained
- How objections are anticipated
- How transitions feel
- How confidence and uncertainty are expressed
- How dense ideas remain understandable
- Which connectors command forms question forms number forms and sentence structures belong to the requested spoken register
- Which otherwise correct forms sound written formal translated or foreign to that register

Do not extract

- Signature lines
- Repeated catchphrases
- Famous openings
- Personal anecdotes
- Identifiable metaphors
- Recurrent templates
- Presenter theatrics shouting reactions urgency or suspense devices
- Word for word sentences

## Imperfect transcript handling

Assume automated transcripts may contain

- Wrong Arabic words
- Lost English terms
- Incorrect speaker boundaries
- Missing punctuation
- Repeated segments
- Phonetic guesses
- Broken sentence boundaries

Use an imperfect transcript to infer broad flow and human teaching behaviour
Do not adopt a strange word merely because it appears in the transcript
Resolve domain terms from independent practitioner usage
Do not build a spoken residue policy from one automated transcript
Confirm register patterns through multiple clear authentic recordings or reliable native practitioner evidence
Discard unclear passages rather than completing them from imagination

## Competitive learning benchmark

Build `BENCHMARK_MATRIX` for the exact promise rather than the broad topic

Inspect strong current alternatives that are legitimately accessible for analysis

- Authoritative curricula and certification outlines
- Highly regarded practical courses
- Strong free educational series
- Professional competency frameworks
- Current employer client or practitioner expectations

Use public outlines previews documentation independent reviews and lawfully supplied materials
Do not infer lesson quality from popularity price production value or marketing claims
Do not copy protected lesson text examples projects or structure

Record

```yaml
- benchmark:
  name:
  market:
  evidence_available:
  stated_outcome:
  capabilities_covered: []
  practical_evidence: []
  apparent_strengths: []
  unverified_claims: []
  gaps_relevant_to_our_promise: []
```

Then compare the proposed course

- Does it omit a capability repeatedly treated as essential by strong authorities
- Does it explain mechanisms or only provide steps
- Does it preserve the native performance modes required by the promise
- Does it include perception retrieval execution interaction diagnosis correction variation completion and handoff where required
- Does it offer credible practice and evidence of readiness
- Does it adapt globally strong principles to the target market
- Is any claimed advantage real and demonstrable

Benchmarking is a gap detector not an imitation instruction
The course must earn its own architecture from its thesis and capability graph

## Current research and evergreen spoken content

Current research is still required because the map must reflect real tools markets performance environments workflows language use counterpart behaviour and constraints
The teleprompter must not repeat volatile details merely because they were verified during generation

Classify every material finding before it reaches lesson semantics

- `STABLE_CORE` for mechanisms principles and durable professional judgement
- `TRANSFERABLE_METHOD` for a process the learner can reuse when tools or values change
- `ILLUSTRATIVE_ASSUMPTION` for a clearly hypothetical number or condition used only to demonstrate reasoning
- `VOLATILE_CURRENT_DETAIL` for information likely to force rerecording soon

`VOLATILE_CURRENT_DETAIL` includes when applicable

- Exact service prices fees salaries market rates or cost claims
- Subscription prices plan names plan limits free tier limits and temporary offers
- Vendor rankings best tool claims and short lived trends
- Exact interface paths coordinates button locations or temporary feature availability
- Current algorithm behaviour platform rules or specifications likely to change
- Current statistics market shares conversion benchmarks or other dated quantities
- Exact legal policy or compliance thresholds that must be checked at the time of action

Volatile findings may inform internal feasibility scope and examples
They may not enter the final spoken course as current facts
Convert them into a stable principle a decision rule or a method for locating and applying the current authoritative value

A hypothetical number may enter only when the lecturer clearly frames it as an example assumption or calculation input
It must not sound like the current price market norm platform limit or promised result

If the promised task cannot be performed honestly without an exact current value teach how to retrieve and verify that value at the moment of use
If that still leaves the lesson incomplete narrow the promise rather than exporting short lived speech

## Contradiction handling

When sources disagree

1. Check whether they address different versions markets contexts or definitions
2. Prefer primary and current authority for facts
3. Preserve real professional uncertainty when no answer dominates
4. Teach the decision boundary instead of forcing a false universal rule
5. Do not average incompatible claims

## Rights privacy and provenance

Track whether source material may be quoted reused transformed or shown
Default to synthesis rather than copying
Do not expose private client data personal data credentials or confidential business material
Synthetic cases must be labelled internally and must not claim invented results

## Research completeness gate

Research is complete only when it can support

- The course promise
- Every foundational mental model
- Every material capability adapter and real performance stage
- Common failure diagnosis including failed attempts observable symptoms corrections and recovery
- The actual start to completion recovery escalation or handoff episode and its ordinary friction
- Learner customer client stakeholder counterpart system feedback misunderstanding revision access approval and delivery realities when relevant
- Market adaptation
- Professional delivery
- Current specifications that affect internal scope plus a durable method for the learner to verify them when needed
- Terminology used in speech
- Project realism and a compact upload whose completed learner action is visibly represented
- Practitioner reality coverage for every core applied capability adapter and performance stage
- A completed source sufficiency matrix with no material unresolved gap
- A complete evergreen claim filter with no volatile spoken detail
- A competitive coverage check for the exact promised outcome
- A spoken stability decision for every volatile price rate subscription limit ranking interface path statistic feature or policy detail

More sources do not equal better research
Remove low authority repetitive promotional and irrelevant material from the active context
Do not stop because a catalogue or official manual supplied a clean sequence
Continue until the course can explain the real starting state attempts friction failure diagnosis correction and handoff the learner is likely to encounter

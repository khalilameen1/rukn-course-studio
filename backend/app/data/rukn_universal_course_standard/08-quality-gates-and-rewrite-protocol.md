# 08 QUALITY GATES AND REWRITE PROTOCOL

## Quality is gated not averaged

A high total score cannot cancel a fatal defect
Use severity based findings

### FATAL

The course or lesson is unsafe false outside the promise unusable by the audience or structurally unable to produce the outcome
Block export

### SERIOUS

The defect materially weakens capability naturalness market fit assessment or professional use
Require rewrite before export

### MINOR

The defect affects polish consistency or ease but does not invalidate learning
Fix when local and safe

### NOTE

Observation with no required change

## Review order

Review in this order because later polish must not hide earlier failure

1. Truth authority and safety
2. Promise and scope
3. Capability and architecture
4. Invisible episodic progression
5. Depth and practical completeness
6. Market and audience fit
7. Project validity
8. Lesson meaning
9. Natural language and terminology
10. Sequence hooks closes and repetition
11. Teleprompter and document rendering

## Thesis gate

Block when

- End performance is not observable
- Promise exceeds duration access or prerequisites
- Market assumptions are absent or imported without evidence
- Capstone cannot prove the promise
- Tool costs make the course inaccessible to the defined learner
- The course title and actual outcome differ

## Map gate

Block when

- A required capability is uncovered
- A high risk capability is only mentioned
- Duplicate outcomes remain
- Practice begins too late
- Diagnosis correction or delivery is missing from a job ready promise
- Modules form a list rather than a progression
- Project gates do not prove module exits
- Lesson count is inflated by fragments
- Compression removed an essential competence
- Lessons marked as dependent can be swapped without changing capability causality
- The peak is larger or louder but not more integrated independent or accountable

## Lesson meaning gate

Block a lesson when

- The conclusion is generic or obvious without a mechanism
- The learner gains no action decision diagnosis or test
- The title contains all the value
- The example decorates rather than proves
- A recommendation has no conditions
- A claim is unsupported or overgeneralised
- The lesson repeats another outcome
- A tool procedure replaces transferable understanding
- The opening is stronger than the body can justify
- The close manufactures suspense unrelated to the sequence
- The lesson creates continuation by withholding its central answer
- The reel is only setup and gives no complete learner payoff

## Invisible episodic progression gate

Block when

- The course is a list of individually useful reels without an earned causal sequence
- A next lesson is advertised instead of becoming necessary through the current payoff
- Tension comes from invented stories fake urgency fake danger or exaggerated conflict
- The instructor is written as an entertainer actor or reaction personality
- Several lessons use the same dramatic beat pattern
- Difficulty rises only through more words more speed or more content rather than judgement ambiguity integration independence or consequence
- Projects interrupt the sequence without proving a capability turn
- The current lesson answer is delayed or fragmented for retention
- The final peak does not retrieve and combine capabilities accumulated across the course

Do not block a genuinely parallel capability merely because it has no dramatic loop
Block invented dependency as seriously as missing dependency

Do not require overt drama language
A quiet technical explanation can pass when the real problem consequence payoff and next dependency are strong

## Audience gate

Block when

- The first lessons assume unintroduced experience
- The course repeatedly talks down to the learner
- Experienced but non professional learners receive no new judgement
- Prerequisites appear only after they are needed
- Examples depend on money access devices or client types outside the audience model
- Address form changes without product instruction

## Market gate

Block when

- Advice could have been written for any country despite material local differences
- Localisation consists only of names currency or slang
- Western workflow budget or taste is presented as universal
- Weak local convention is presented as professional truth
- Current local facts lack verification
- Market claims infer performance from public visibility alone

## Competitive depth gate

Block when

- No benchmark was performed for a substantial career or professional course
- Strong accessible alternatives repeatedly cover a required capability our map omits
- The course is longer but not deeper more practical or better adapted
- A claimed advantage rests on branding format or hooks rather than learning value
- Benchmark material has been copied instead of used to detect gaps

Do not require every reel to outperform every public video
Require the complete course to fulfil its stated promise with stronger connected capability evidence than generic fragmented alternatives

## Language and terminology gate

Block when

- Speech sounds translated
- Formal and colloquial grammar mix materially
- Written register residue remains in syntax morphology connectors word order commands questions number forms or inflection even when every individual word is common
- A profile specific residue candidate remains unresolved after contextual review
- A literal translation can mislead or sound insulting strange or unknown in the field
- An English term is used for display rather than actual professional usage
- A domain term changes across lessons without reason
- Corporate compliance language replaces practical teaching
- A line requires hidden punctuation to be understood
- A sentence is split into visual fragments
- Repeated verbal tics make the instructor sound generated
- A language repair changes a claim condition exception cause sequence contrast emphasis example action level of certainty or continuation dependency
- Language repairs concentrate a small set of colloquial connectors into an audible course wide habit

## Project gate

Block when

- The project can pass by copying
- Inputs tools rights or instructions are unclear
- Rubric criteria are subjective labels without observable evidence
- The project does not require the module capability
- No inspect revise loop exists where quality matters
- A simulated case claims real performance
- The capstone is only a larger exercise
- Portfolio claims cannot be substantiated

## Instructor demonstration and asset gate

Block when

- The lecturer claims personal work results clients credentials or experience absent from `INSTRUCTOR_PROFILE`
- A demonstration lesson cannot be performed as written
- The script describes a visible result absent from the demonstration plan
- A required asset is missing inaccessible unlicensed private or in the wrong start state
- Project instructions depend on a supporting brief that does not exist
- The learner cannot reproduce the exercise from the supplied inputs

## Course wide repetition gate

Run semantic and phrase level checks across all lessons

Inspect

- Duplicate capability outcomes
- Repeated claims and examples
- Repeated opening first words
- Repeated hook types
- Repeated negation title formulas
- Repeated transitions
- Repeated closing shapes
- Repeated suspense reveal urgency shock or reaction language
- A prior close copied into a later opening
- The same misconception stretched across multiple reels
- Summary lessons that add no integration

Exact text similarity is insufficient
Review semantic overlap
Two lessons may use different words and still teach the same thing

## Practical completeness gate

For a career course test a realistic first task from intake through delivery against the course

Record every step the learner must perform
If the task requires an unintroduced decision tool foundation diagnosis or handoff add it or narrow the promise

Do not use course length as evidence of completeness

## Independent adversarial final review

Before export run three separated review passes on the accepted course

### Domain reality pass

Look for false claims missing professional steps unrealistic cases tool fantasy and weak diagnosis

### Learning architecture pass

Look for hidden prerequisites superficial coverage broken progression weak projects disconnected useful reels artificial continuation and a capstone that fails to prove the promise

### Spoken human pass

Run this pass after every other accepted rewrite

First read for translated language written register residue formal colloquial drift unnatural terminology repeated formulas fake instructor experience and teleprompter fragments
Review complete sentences and continuous lesson flow rather than isolated tokens

Then compare every language rewrite with its accepted semantic record
Confirm that claims conditions exceptions sequence contrast emphasis examples learner actions and earned next dependencies remain intact

Finally inspect the phrase ledger for repair induced repetition across lessons modules and the complete course

Each pass begins from the accepted artefacts and ledgers rather than the writer justification
Apply every fatal or serious finding through the rewrite protocol

Do not expose the course to the user until all three passes and the render pass return `PASS`

## Three reel writer probe

Before expensive full generation use a representative three reel probe when validating a new writer configuration

Choose

- One conceptual or diagnostic lesson
- One execution or screen lesson
- One tradeoff case or professional lesson

Use the same language profile terminology market pack creator prompt and lesson review stack intended for the full course
Pass selected sources needed by those lessons

The probe tests

- Meaning density
- Natural voice
- Terminology
- Hook and close variety
- Complete lesson payoff and earned next dependency
- Capability based escalation without theatrical performance
- Teleprompter formatting
- Domain depth

It does not certify

- Map quality
- Project progression
- Whole course repetition
- Full source coverage
- Export readiness

During software development do not generate a complete paid course merely to test plumbing
Use deterministic fixtures for structure and the smallest representative paid probe for writing quality

## Reviewer output contract

Every finding contains

```yaml
finding:
  severity:
  location:
  violated_rule:
  evidence:
  cause:
  required_change:
  rewrite_scope:
```

Do not return vague feedback such as improve the hook make it more natural add depth or reduce repetition
Identify the missing meaning unnatural term duplicated outcome or false assumption

## Rewrite protocol

Rewrite at the layer that caused the defect

### Source defect

Research or verify again
Do not rewrite around uncertainty

### Thesis defect

Repair the promise audience or scope then remap affected capabilities

### Map defect

Merge split reorder add or remove lesson outcomes before prose rewrite

### Meaning defect

Replace the semantic payload example mechanism or learner action

### Language defect

Freeze the accepted semantic record then rewrite directly in the target spoken variety
Compare the pre rewrite and post rewrite meaning field by field
Update the phrase ledger and inspect for repair induced repetition

### Teleprompter defect

Rewrite sentence boundaries then render again
Do not insert arbitrary line breaks

Use the smallest rewrite scope that fully removes the cause
After a rewrite rerun the affected local gates and all relevant course wide gates

## Attempt policy

Do not loop blindly through repeated rewrites

After two failed rewrites of the same lesson

1. Diagnose whether the map source or thesis is wrong
2. Stop regenerating prose
3. Repair the upstream layer
4. Generate the lesson again from corrected semantics

## Deterministic checks

Automate checks where reliable

- Missing or empty lessons
- Word ranges by delivery mode
- Exact duplicate lines
- High text overlap
- Repeated opening n grams
- Repeated teaser urgency suspense reveal or exaggerated reaction patterns
- Forbidden metadata labels
- Punctuation in spoken body
- Inconsistent address form indicators
- Profile specific written register residue candidates including candidates carrying an attached one letter prefix
- Formal morphology conditional result command question dual case and number patterns selected for the active language profile
- Concentration of transition words and conversational particles by lesson module and course
- Missing module projects
- Missing required terms or capabilities
- Current claims without ledger records
- DOCX package validity
- Page count and rendering failures

Do not pretend automated checks can judge depth naturalness truth or market fit alone
Those require editorial review grounded in the ledgers
Candidate matches are review triggers rather than universal errors unless the active language profile explicitly marks a form forbidden
Normalise diacritics and surrounding punctuation for detection without changing protected quotations technical tokens code or target language examples

## Export blockers

Export is forbidden while any of these remains

- Fatal or serious finding
- Empty or placeholder text
- Unsupported material claim
- Unresolved literal terminology
- Final spoken variety integrity gate not passed after the last content rewrite
- Unresolved written register residue in ordinary lecturer speech
- A language rewrite that has not passed semantic preservation comparison
- Repair induced transition repetition that remains audibly formulaic
- Semantic duplicate lesson
- Artificial cliffhanger answer withholding or a lesson with no complete payoff
- Sequence progression dependent on theatrical performance rather than capability causality
- Missing project gate
- Missing capstone evidence
- Teleprompter sentence fragments
- Metadata or reviewer leakage
- Unreadable mixed direction text
- Unrendered or visually uninspected final document
- Any textual or language edit made after the version that was rendered and inspected
- Missing competitive benchmark when required
- Unverified instructor claim
- Missing demonstration plan or required asset

Scoring is diagnostic only
Pass is determined by blockers

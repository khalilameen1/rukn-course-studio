# 08 QUALITY GATES AND REWRITE PROTOCOL

## Quality is gated not averaged

A high total score cannot cancel a fatal defect
Use severity based findings

### FATAL

The course or lesson is unsafe false outside the promise unusable by the audience or structurally unable to produce the outcome
Block export

### SERIOUS

The defect materially weakens capability naturalness market fit project usefulness or professional use
Require rewrite before export

### MINOR

The defect affects polish consistency or ease but does not invalidate learning
Fix when local and safe

### NOTE

Observation with no required change

## Review order

Review in this order because later polish must not hide earlier failure

1. Truth authority and safety
2. Source role separation and practitioner reality
3. Promise and scope
4. Capability composition adapter fit and context overlays
5. Real performance episode workflow capability and architecture
6. Duration sufficiency and reel boundaries
7. Invisible episodic progression
8. Depth and practical completeness
9. Market and audience fit
10. Inter module project inspectability and final boundary readiness
11. Module boundary bridge validity
12. Lesson meaning
13. Natural language and terminology
14. Sequence hooks closes and repetition
15. Evergreen spoken content and output purity
16. Teleprompter and document rendering

## Thesis gate

Block when

- End performance is not observable
- Promise exceeds duration access or prerequisites
- Market assumptions are absent or imported without evidence
- The final boundary integrated readiness performance cannot prove the promise
- The final module would introduce a new promise critical standalone capability after formal project proof has ended
- Tool costs make the course inaccessible to the defined learner
- A client or job ready promise has no start to handoff workflow boundary
- A non business promise has no authentic performance episode from first input or stimulus through completion correction escalation or transfer
- Non final module projects cannot produce a readable platform supported attachment that exposes the completed capability directly
- A planned attachment cannot pass self evidence inspectability without hidden context external access unseen process or an evaluator assumption
- The course title and actual outcome differ

## Capability composition gate

Block when

- Architecture was selected from the course title industry or one broad family label
- `COURSE_PERFORMANCE_COMPOSITION` `PERFORMANCE_EPISODE_MAP` `CAPABILITY_ADAPTER_MAP` or `CAPABILITY_MODE_MATRIX` is missing
- A promised situation trigger input perception decision interaction action failure correction completion or handoff is absent from the episode map
- A required capability has no accepted primary adapter
- Supporting adapters required for valid real performance were discarded for simplicity
- Secondary adapters were added merely because their terminology appears rather than because their native action is required
- A mixed course receives one generic lesson form practice cycle or attachment type
- Language accuracy is treated as proof of job workflow listening adaptation diagnosis tool use documentation or handoff
- Job knowledge or a written script is treated as proof of target language comprehension production pronunciation register timing or live adaptation
- Subject explanation or a lesson plan is treated as proof of teaching elicitation diagnosis correction retry and transfer
- Rule recall is treated as proof of perceptual discrimination quality inspection or action on the detected signal
- Tool completion is treated as proof of judgement interaction recovery or professional completion
- A knowledge course is forced into artificial portfolio client or production conventions
- An unfamiliar capability is forced into a weak reusable adapter or generic explanation
- A core custom adapter remains provisional lacks practitioner support or has no performance loop correction transfer independence and honest evidence contract
- A required authority safety privacy tool stakeholder multilingual access or volatility overlay is absent

Reopen the thesis research or composition layer before module mapping
Do not repair a composition failure by adding generic exercises after the architecture is already written

## Source reality gate

Block a substantial practical applied interactive teaching perceptual operational creative analytical technical physical job oriented or client facing course when

- The active source set is dominated by catalogues manuals curricula vendor pages or polished success examples
- `PRACTITIONER_REALITY_PACK` is missing for a core practical capability
- A real performance or workflow stage has no evidence of ordinary starting conditions inputs signals variation friction failures correction completion or handoff
- A capability adapter or custom adapter loop has no practitioner performance evidence
- One anecdote reply creator or community is treated as universal practice
- Forum group or community claims are used as technical factual legal safety or platform authority without verification
- Research excludes failed attempts disagreements edge cases and corrections because they are less polished
- Private or closed community material is used without authorised access provenance privacy protection or synthesis
- Practitioner wording stories or identifiable client details are copied into the course
- `SOURCE_SUFFICIENCY_MATRIX` contains a material unresolved gap
- Research stops at source count before practical saturation is reached

Authoritative documentation remains mandatory where it is the proper fact source
The gate requires complementary roles rather than replacing authority with anecdotes

## Map gate

Block when

- A required capability is uncovered
- A required performance episode adapter assignment context overlay or native learning cycle is uncovered
- A required real performance stage or action is absent from `PERFORMANCE_EPISODE_MAP` or `REAL_WORKFLOW_TRACE`
- `CAPABILITY_ADAPTER_MAP` or `CAPABILITY_MODE_MATRIX` has a material unresolved row
- A workflow row depends on an unintroduced action asset access permission communication or handoff
- A high risk capability is only mentioned
- Duplicate outcomes remain
- Practice begins too late
- Diagnosis correction or delivery is missing from a job ready promise
- Modules form a list rather than a progression
- Project gates do not prove standard module exits
- The final boundary project does not integrate the promise critical capability set or the final module creates a new proof gap
- Lesson count is inflated by fragments
- Compression removed an essential competence or workflow glue step
- The cold start walkthrough reaches a missing middle
- Lessons marked as dependent can be swapped without changing capability causality
- The peak is larger or louder but not more integrated independent or accountable


## Duration sufficiency gate

Block when

- Total duration or reel count was selected before the capability graph workflow trace demonstrations and practice needs
- The course is approximately forty minutes or another short runtime only because it was forced into a small reel count while the promise requires executable work
- A small count is defended as concise even though execution diagnosis correction client reality or delivery is missing
- A large count is defended as comprehensive even though reels are fragments duplicates or filler
- A standalone teaching reel is estimated below 60 seconds or above 300 seconds
- Word count is used to hide a screen demonstration that will exceed five minutes
- A required capability is compressed into general advice to meet a marketing duration
- The map is padded to reach the 120 minute planning anchor
- `DURATION_BUDGET` does not account for demonstration runtime workflow glue project explanation and required variation

Approximately 120 spoken minutes is a planning anchor rather than a pass condition
No default reel count ceiling exists
Pass only when duration is sufficient for the exact promise and every reel represents one complete meaningful capability change

## Lesson meaning gate

Block a lesson when

- The conclusion is generic or obvious without a mechanism
- The learner gains no perception retrieval action response decision diagnosis correction or test
- The lesson ignores the native modelling practice feedback correction or transfer requirement of its accepted adapter
- The title contains all the value
- The example decorates rather than proves
- A recommendation has no conditions
- A claim is unsupported or overgeneralised
- The lesson repeats another outcome
- A tool procedure replaces transferable understanding
- The opening is stronger than the body can justify
- The close manufactures suspense unrelated to the sequence
- The lesson creates continuation by withholding its central answer
- The reel is only setup and gives no complete learner payoff

## Invisible episodic progression gate

Block when

- The course is a list of individually useful reels without an earned causal sequence
- A next lesson is advertised instead of becoming necessary through the current payoff
- Tension comes from invented stories fake urgency fake danger or exaggerated conflict
- The instructor is written as an entertainer actor or reaction personality
- Several lessons use the same dramatic beat pattern
- Difficulty rises only through more words more speed or more content rather than judgement ambiguity integration independence or consequence
- Projects interrupt the sequence without proving a capability turn
- A non final module boundary lacks an earned close after its project
- A module bridge names or advertises the following module instead of exposing a real work dependency
- A module bridge withholds a current answer uses fake suspense or repeats one formula across modules
- The current lesson answer is delayed or fragmented for retention
- The final boundary integrated readiness project does not retrieve and combine the promise critical capabilities accumulated across the course

Do not block a genuinely parallel capability merely because it has no dramatic loop
Block invented dependency as seriously as missing dependency

Do not require overt drama language
A quiet technical explanation can pass when the real problem consequence payoff and next dependency are strong

## Audience gate

Block when

- The first lessons assume unintroduced experience
- The course repeatedly talks down to the learner
- Experienced but non professional learners receive no new judgement
- Prerequisites appear only after they are needed
- Examples depend on money access devices or client types outside the audience model
- Address form changes without product instruction

## Market gate

Block when

- Advice could have been written for any country despite material local differences
- Localisation consists only of names currency or slang
- Western workflow budget or taste is presented as universal
- Weak local convention is presented as professional truth
- Current local facts lack verification
- Market claims infer performance from public visibility alone

## Evergreen spoken content and output purity gate

Block when

- The spoken course states an exact current service price market rate salary fee subscription amount plan limit discount or temporary offer
- A hypothetical numerical example can be mistaken for a current market fact platform value or promised result
- A current ranking best tool claim short lived trend algorithm tactic feature announcement or dated statistic is used as durable teaching
- A fragile interface path button position menu coordinate or current version detail is necessary for the sentence to remain correct
- A current policy legal platform or technical threshold is recorded instead of teaching how to verify and apply the current authoritative value
- The script uses today currently this year latest or equivalent time anchoring to make advice that will expire
- A direct link must remain unchanged for the lesson to work
- Research verification date is used as permission to place a volatile value in the recording
- A visible line is not lecturer speech or an allowed course module reel or project heading
- Internal map labels sources citations review notes production directions platform operations schemas timing or metadata appear in the teleprompter
- A project describes anything beyond the learner action and upload instructions
- An unlabelled module bridge is exported with a bridge teaser cliffhanger or next module heading
- `EVERGREEN_CLAIM_FILTER` contains an unresolved volatile item

Rewrite volatile detail into a stable principle decision method verification routine or clearly hypothetical teaching example
Remove any visible line the lecturer will not say

## Competitive depth gate

Block when

- No benchmark was performed for a substantial career or professional course
- Strong accessible alternatives repeatedly cover a required capability our map omits
- The course is longer but not deeper more practical or better adapted
- A claimed advantage rests on branding format or hooks rather than learning value
- Benchmark material has been copied instead of used to detect gaps

Do not require every reel to outperform every public video
Require the complete course to fulfil its stated promise with stronger connected capability evidence than generic fragmented alternatives

## Language and terminology gate

Block when

- Speech sounds translated
- Formal and colloquial grammar mix materially
- Written register residue remains in syntax morphology connectors word order commands questions number forms or inflection even when every individual word is common
- A profile specific residue candidate remains unresolved after contextual review
- A literal translation can mislead or sound insulting strange or unknown in the field
- An English term is used for display rather than actual professional usage
- A domain term changes across lessons without reason
- Corporate compliance language replaces practical teaching
- A line requires hidden punctuation to be understood
- A sentence is split into visual fragments
- Repeated verbal tics make the instructor sound generated
- A language repair changes a claim condition exception cause sequence contrast emphasis example action level of certainty or continuation dependency
- Language repairs concentrate a small set of colloquial connectors into an audible course wide habit

## Project gate

Block when

- A non final module has no project after its reels
- A project appears after the final module
- The project cannot produce a readable image screenshot or supported file whose decisive evidence is directly inspectable
- The attachment self evidence check is missing fails or depends on hidden context external access unseen process unsupported parsing or a long explanation
- The internal inspectability record contains a rubric evaluator prompt score threshold feedback path or post upload behaviour
- The project can be completed by copying or submitting an unchanged supplied example
- The situation learner action supplied inputs essential constraint upload type or visible result is unclear
- A standard project tests many loosely connected lessons instead of one dominant exit capability or the smallest inseparable cluster
- The final boundary project combines unrelated tasks instead of one connected integrated performance episode
- Expected active effort exceeds thirty minutes without redesign review and a capability based reason
- The project requires a real client external account paid spend public response external approval lucky input or multi day result
- More than three attachments are required without evidence that fewer cannot expose the capability
- The attachment shows decoration while the capability depends on hidden structure timing runtime interaction or another unavailable property
- A static image is treated as proof of live spoken timed interactive or physical performance
- The project introduces a concept tool research task or workflow step not taught before the boundary
- The relevant result can be understood only through a long explanation of invisible work
- The project asks for an output but the module taught no way to inspect or correct that output
- The spoken project text switches audience away from the learner or describes what happens after upload
- The lecturer names the mechanism that will process or route the attachment
- The spoken project instruction becomes another lesson exceeds five minutes or needs a separate instruction brief to be understandable
- The project duplicates another boundary at the same capability level
- The last project before the final module is not a compact integrated readiness proof
- The final integrated readiness project omits any promise critical capability or most core capability integration
- The final module introduces a new promise critical standalone capability after the last formal proof
- The later module depends on competence the project does not reveal
- A simulated case is presented as a real client result

The internal design may record the capability and visible result needed to keep the task clear
Do not expose that internal check in learner facing text

## Module boundary bridge gate

Block when

- A non final module has no unlabelled bridge after its project
- The bridge is placed before the project or inside the final reel instead of being the last spoken paragraph before the next module heading
- The bridge fails to complete the capability earned in the current module
- The exposed limitation consequence tradeoff or work pressure is not a real dependency for later learning
- The bridge says in the next module next part coming up later we will see or an equivalent explicit preview
- The bridge names advertises or summarises the following module
- The bridge withholds an answer the completed module owes
- The bridge uses fake danger urgency mystery drama or an empty suspense question
- The bridge asks the project to use an untaught capability
- The next module opening does not resolve the dependency the bridge exposed
- Several module boundaries repeat the same sentence shape or causal device
- The bridge exceeds three natural sentences without a capability reason
- The final module has an open forward bridge or an unnecessary project instead of a complete resolution

A quiet technical close passes when the current capability is complete and its real limit makes later work necessary

## Instructor demonstration and asset gate

Block when

- The lecturer claims personal work results clients credentials or experience absent from `INSTRUCTOR_PROFILE`
- A demonstration lesson cannot be performed as written
- The script describes a visible result absent from the demonstration plan
- A required asset is missing inaccessible unlicensed private or in the wrong start state
- Project instructions depend on any separately generated supporting brief checklist or workbook
- The learner cannot reproduce the exercise from the supplied inputs

## Course wide repetition gate

Run semantic and phrase level checks across all lessons

Inspect

- Duplicate capability outcomes
- Repeated claims and examples
- Repeated opening first words
- Repeated hook types
- Repeated negation title formulas
- Repeated transitions
- Repeated closing shapes
- Repeated suspense reveal urgency shock or reaction language
- A prior close copied into a later opening
- The same misconception stretched across multiple reels
- Summary lessons that add no integration

Exact text similarity is insufficient
Review semantic overlap
Two lessons may use different words and still teach the same thing

## Practical completeness gate

For every applied promise run the no missing middle audit on a realistic first performance episode from the authentic trigger input stimulus request call case learner response or brief through completion correction escalation transfer delivery or handoff

Record every perception retrieval action decision phrase response input asset access permission interaction check failure correction recovery documentation version escalation completion export and handoff the learner must perform
For each step identify the exact lesson demonstration practice project or pre existing supplied asset that prepares the learner before first use

Block when

- The central subject or craft is taught but perception retrieval setup access interaction scope communication feedback correction documentation completion delivery or recovery required by the promise is assumed
- A phrase such as prepare the files handle the client set everything up or deliver professionally bridges an untrained action
- The learner must discover a tool state file convention approval path or quality check during the first real task
- A supplied asset is required but absent incomplete rights restricted or in the wrong start state
- The course includes a workflow stage only as a title mention without execution decision evidence or recovery

If a required step is unintroduced add it
If it is outside scope narrow the promise and the simulated task so the learner never depends on it

Do not use course length as evidence of completeness
Use the workflow coverage matrix and cold start walkthrough

## Independent adversarial final review

Before export run four separated review passes on the accepted course

### Domain reality pass

Look for false claims catalogue only teaching missing real performance steps unrealistic clean starting states tool fantasy weak diagnosis absent failed attempts counterpart friction completion and handoff gaps

### Capability composition pass

Ignore the course title and reconstruct the required performance from the promise
Check that every capability has the correct primary adapter supporting adapters overlays native practice correction transfer and evidence
Look specifically for language courses that omit job performance job courses that omit language performance teaching courses that omit learner response and correction sales or service courses that become scripts and unfamiliar domains reduced to generic explanation

### Learning architecture pass

Look for hidden prerequisites superficial coverage broken progression weak projects disconnected useful reels artificial continuation weak module bridges and a final integrated performance that fails to prove the promise

### Spoken human pass

Run this pass after every other accepted rewrite

First read for translated language written register residue formal colloquial drift unnatural terminology repeated formulas fake instructor experience and teleprompter fragments
Review complete sentences and continuous lesson flow rather than isolated tokens

Then compare every language rewrite with its accepted semantic record
Confirm that claims conditions exceptions sequence contrast emphasis examples learner actions and earned next dependencies remain intact

Finally inspect the phrase ledger for repair induced repetition across lessons modules and the complete course

Each pass begins from the accepted artefacts and ledgers rather than the writer justification
Apply every fatal or serious finding through the rewrite protocol

Do not expose the course to the user until all four passes and the render pass return `PASS`

## Three reel writer probe

Before expensive full generation use a representative three reel probe when validating a new writer configuration

Choose

- One conceptual or diagnostic lesson
- One execution or screen lesson
- One tradeoff case or professional lesson

Use the same language profile terminology market pack creator prompt and lesson review stack intended for the full course
Pass selected sources needed by those lessons

The probe tests

- Meaning density
- Natural voice
- Terminology
- Hook and close variety
- Complete lesson payoff and earned next dependency
- Capability based escalation without theatrical performance
- Teleprompter formatting
- Domain depth

It does not certify

- Map quality
- Project progression
- Whole course repetition
- Full source coverage
- Export readiness

During software development do not generate a complete paid course merely to test plumbing
Use deterministic fixtures for structure and the smallest representative paid probe for writing quality

## Reviewer output contract

Every finding contains

```yaml
finding:
  severity:
  location:
  violated_rule:
  evidence:
  cause:
  required_change:
  rewrite_scope:
```

Do not return vague feedback such as improve the hook make it more natural add depth or reduce repetition
Identify the missing meaning unnatural term duplicated outcome or false assumption

## Rewrite protocol

Rewrite at the layer that caused the defect

### Source defect

Research or verify again
Do not rewrite around uncertainty

### Thesis defect

Repair the promise audience or scope then remap affected capabilities

### Map defect

Merge split reorder add or remove lesson outcomes before prose rewrite

### Meaning defect

Replace the semantic payload example mechanism or learner action

### Language defect

Freeze the accepted semantic record then rewrite directly in the target spoken variety
Compare the pre rewrite and post rewrite meaning field by field
Update the phrase ledger and inspect for repair induced repetition

### Teleprompter defect

Rewrite sentence boundaries then render again
Do not insert arbitrary line breaks

Use the smallest rewrite scope that fully removes the cause
After a rewrite rerun the affected local gates and all relevant course wide gates

## Attempt policy

Do not loop blindly through repeated rewrites

After two failed rewrites of the same lesson

1. Diagnose whether the map source or thesis is wrong
2. Stop regenerating prose
3. Repair the upstream layer
4. Generate the lesson again from corrected semantics

## Deterministic checks

Automate checks where reliable

- Missing or empty lessons
- Spoken reel duration below 60 seconds or above 300 seconds
- Word ranges by delivery mode as secondary duration signals
- Exact duplicate lines
- High text overlap
- Repeated opening n grams
- Repeated teaser urgency suspense reveal or exaggerated reaction patterns
- Forbidden metadata labels
- Punctuation in spoken body
- Inconsistent address form indicators
- Profile specific written register residue candidates including candidates carrying an attached one letter prefix
- Formal morphology conditional result command question dual case and number patterns selected for the active language profile
- Concentration of transition words and conversational particles by lesson module and course
- Missing non final module projects or any project after the final module
- Missing or misplaced module bridge closes
- Explicit next module teaser language in module bridges
- Platform or post upload operations in learner facing projects
- Missing inter module project fields attachment types learner action visible result or compactness evidence
- Missing failed or evaluator expanded attachment self evidence record
- Missing final boundary integrated readiness designation capability coverage or final module resolution contract
- Checkpoint effort above the policy review threshold without an approved capability based reason
- Required performance or workflow rows without mapped teaching practice correction project proof or assets
- Missing capability adapter assignments context overlays or accepted custom adapter status
- Missing required terms or capabilities
- Volatile spoken candidates including prices subscriptions rates rankings limits interface routes current statistics and temporary feature claims
- More than one user visible course artefact
- Generated companion brief workbook map source pack or report
- DOCX package validity
- Page count and rendering failures

Do not pretend automated checks can judge depth naturalness truth or market fit alone
Those require editorial review grounded in the ledgers
Candidate matches are review triggers rather than universal errors unless the active language profile explicitly marks a form forbidden
Normalise diacritics and surrounding punctuation for detection without changing protected quotations technical tokens code or target language examples

## Export blockers

Export is forbidden while any of these remains

- Fatal or serious finding
- Empty or placeholder text
- Unsupported material claim
- Unresolved literal terminology
- Final spoken variety integrity gate not passed after the last content rewrite
- Unresolved written register residue in ordinary lecturer speech
- A language rewrite that has not passed semantic preservation comparison
- Repair induced transition repetition that remains audibly formulaic
- Semantic duplicate lesson
- Artificial cliffhanger answer withholding or a lesson with no complete payoff
- Sequence progression dependent on theatrical performance rather than capability causality
- Missing non final module project
- A project whose completed capability is not directly inspectable in a supported attachment
- A missing or failed attachment self evidence check or any generated evaluator grading feedback or post upload logic
- An excessive or externally dependent module project
- A project that relies on hidden work external access or an unreadable result
- Learner facing project text contains platform or post upload operations
- Missing misplaced explicit or formulaic module bridge close
- An open forward bridge or any project after the final module instead of complete resolution
- The last project before the final module is not the accepted compact integrated readiness proof
- A promise critical capability is absent from that proof or first appears in the final module
- The final module does not use the accepted integrated work or exists only as recap
- Final integrated performance does not prove the course promise
- Any unresolved volatile price subscription rate ranking plan limit interface path current statistic temporary feature or similar short lived spoken detail
- Any visible line that is not lecturer speech or an allowed heading
- More than one user visible course file or any generated companion document beside the lecturer DOCX
- Missing practitioner reality pack source sufficiency or adapter evidence for a core applied capability
- Missing or unresolved performance episode map capability adapter map capability mode matrix context overlay or custom adapter contract
- Missing required real workflow stage or unresolved no missing middle finding
- A teaching reel outside the one to five minute boundary
- A duration budget that preserves a large promise through superficial coverage
- Teleprompter sentence fragments
- Metadata or reviewer leakage
- Unreadable mixed direction text
- Unrendered or visually uninspected final document
- Any textual or language edit made after the version that was rendered and inspected
- Missing competitive benchmark when required
- Unverified instructor claim
- Missing demonstration plan or required asset

Scoring is diagnostic only
Pass is determined by blockers

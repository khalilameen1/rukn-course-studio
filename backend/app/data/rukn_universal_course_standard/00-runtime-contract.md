# 00 RUNTIME CONTRACT

## Mission

Build a Rukn course that transforms a defined learner into a person who can perform a defined real world skill at a defined level

Do not build a catalogue of topics
Do not build a long social media thread
Do not build a motivational product
Do not build documentation read aloud
Do not build a tool button tour unless the promise is explicitly tool operation and the action cannot be taught more durably
Do not build LMS functionality

The course is a guided sequence of perception retrieval decisions demonstrations practice interaction diagnosis correction transfer and complete real performance
The production process may use extensive internal planning
The final course product is one filming ready lecturer DOCX only

## Rukn invisible episodic principle

Rukn transfers the structural intelligence of short drama into learning without transferring theatrical performance

Use

- Causal reel to reel progression
- Meaningful tension produced by a real decision failure constraint tradeoff or consequence
- A complete payoff inside every lesson
- A newly earned need that makes the following lesson necessary
- Accumulated capability that raises difficulty judgement and independence toward a genuine peak
- A natural module closure after each non final project that exposes the next real performance condition

Do not use

- Acting shouting exaggerated reactions or an entertainer persona
- Fake stories fake urgency fake stakes or manufactured conflict
- Artificial cliffhangers
- Withholding the answer of the current lesson to force continuation
- Announcements such as in the next reel or in the next module
- Editing gimmicks or hyperactive phrasing as a substitute for meaning
- A repeated dramatic beat formula
- MrBeast style presentation or imitation of entertainment creators

The architecture should be felt as momentum but remain invisible in the lecturer speech
The learner should continue because the completed answer exposes the next real problem not because the instructor performs suspense

At module scale the project gives the learner a small proof action
The last project before the final module is the compact integrated readiness proof
It combines the promise critical capability set at a bounded scale and does not become a long capstone
The final spoken lines of each project should close the achieved capability then reveal one honest limit changed condition or consequence that makes continued learning desirable
Do not label this as a bridge or cliffhanger in the DOCX

## Primary optimisation target

Optimise in this order

1. Safety and verified truth
2. Learner capability after practice
3. Depth and usefulness of meaning
4. Coherent progression and transfer to the target context
5. Natural spoken delivery
6. Long recording life and resistance to avoidable expiry
7. Attention and shareability
8. Brevity

Never trade a higher priority for a lower one
A striking hook cannot rescue a shallow lesson
A natural voice cannot rescue an incorrect term
A large map cannot rescue a false promise
A current fact cannot justify making the recording expire quickly when the same capability can be taught durably

## Universal capability composition

This standard may generate a course in any domain
Do not choose the architecture from the title industry or a single course family
Derive the promised real performance then classify every required capability by what the learner must actually do

Course level family labels are optional summaries
They do not control lesson form practice project evidence or progression
Build `COURSE_PERFORMANCE_COMPOSITION` and `PERFORMANCE_EPISODE_MAP` before capability assignment
The canonical capability level control is `CAPABILITY_ADAPTER_MAP` with `CAPABILITY_MODE_MATRIX` as its coverage and proof view

A capability may primarily require one of these recurring modes

- Knowledge retrieval interpretation or explanation
- Perceptual discrimination and quality inspection
- Creation and production
- Operational process and case handling
- Measured marketing or media operation
- Sales service and live interaction
- Language comprehension or production
- Teaching facilitation or coaching
- Technical system building operation or debugging
- Analytical judgement and decision
- Physical or embodied performance

Authority safety legal ethical privacy compliance and tool requirements act as overlays across any mode
More than one mode may be inseparable inside one capability
Select one primary adapter and only the secondary adapters actually needed for that capability

The list is open ended
When no listed adapter fits derive a custom adapter from the observable action authentic inputs decisions signals failures correction loop transfer conditions independence standard and honest project evidence
Mark it provisional until practitioner evidence supports the performance loop then store the accepted contract in `CUSTOM_ADAPTER_REGISTRY`
Do not collapse an unfamiliar field into explanation only

Examples of composition

- Sales commonly combines knowledge live interaction perceptual signals analytical judgement operational follow up and compliance
- Language for call centre work commonly combines language listening discrimination live interaction case handling system operation documentation escalation and privacy boundaries
- Teaching an authoritative subject to non native speakers commonly combines source accuracy language teaching facilitation pronunciation discrimination and adaptive interaction
- Design commonly combines creation visual inspection analytical choice tool operation revision and delivery

Adapt lesson modes demonstrations practice and project evidence at capability level
Do not force every capability into the same module shape lesson method or attachment type
Load the detailed adapter contract in `12-course-family-adapters.md` before architecture

## Runtime phases

Execute phases in order

1. Resolve intake and uncertainty
2. Build the observable promise and provisional real performance episode composition
3. Decompose episodes into capability units with provisional primary adapters supporting adapters and overlays
4. Research facts practitioner reality market language voice and native performance patterns separately
5. Refine and accept the capability adapter map custom adapter registry and performance episode map
6. Benchmark the promised competence against strong current alternatives
7. Build the real performance or workflow trace capability graph capability mode matrix coverage matrices and duration budget
8. Design the course map compact module project spine final boundary readiness proof demonstration needs asset needs and module closure bridges
9. Build and stress test the invisible episodic progression map
10. Stress test the map for promise scope adapter fit depth real performance completeness final proof coverage project feasibility attachment self evidence evergreen durability and duration sufficiency
11. Write lessons meaning first using each capability native learning cycle
12. Verify every planned project attachment through `ATTACHMENT_SELF_EVIDENCE_CHECK` without generating grading evaluator feedback or post upload logic
13. Write standard projects as learner instructions using evidence native to the dominant exit capability and write the final boundary project from the accepted integrated readiness record
14. Apply spoken language and teleprompter pass
15. Run lesson module whole course and domain neutrality gates and apply every accepted rewrite
16. Run an independent final spoken variety residue evergreen leakage and semantic preservation pass
17. Rerun affected composition meaning repetition teleprompter and render gates
18. Export one final DOCX only after all blockers pass

Do not draft lessons before the thesis performance episode map accepted capability adapter map capability mode matrix capability graph project spine and module closure logic are stable
Do not use later prose quality to hide an architectural failure

## Internal two layer rule

Maintain two layers during generation

### Planning layer

May contain evidence citations visual plans teaching notes source provenance terms claims project proof design final boundary readiness logic attachment self evidence checks map logic and reviewer findings
It may also contain machine readable records used to verify completeness

It must not expand into a separate user deliverable
Keep LMS behavior outside the course authoring product

### Learner facing layer

Contains only what the lecturer is meant to say plus the allowed course module reel and project headings
It contains the project action and upload instruction and stops there
It never contains a rubric evaluator prompt scoring rule feedback path model instruction or post upload behaviour

Never leak planning labels into the learner facing layer
Never weaken internal verification because citations and notes are absent from the final document
Never export the planning layer beside the DOCX
This authoring run has one product and that product is the lecturer DOCX

## Meaning before wording

For every lesson complete these passes in order

1. Determine the exact capability change
2. Determine the non obvious useful meaning
3. Select proof example demonstration or test
4. Determine the action or decision the learner can now make
5. Confirm the lesson gives a complete payoff
6. Confirm any next need is earned by a real newly exposed dependency
7. Remove or transform fast expiring claims before prose
8. Write the spoken explanation
9. Write the opening from the strongest meaning
10. Write the close from the unresolved next dependency
11. Apply natural speech and teleprompter formatting

Do not begin by inventing hooks
Do not polish sentences while the lesson still lacks a worthwhile idea

For a module boundary complete the project first then write the closure bridge from the real limit exposed by the project result
Do not write the bridge as a promotional teaser

## First request final result contract

After sufficient brief sources and product decisions are available run all drafting reviewing rewriting course wide checking exporting and rendering passes internally

Do not expose a first draft as the deliverable
Do not ask the user to rediscover a defect already detectable by this standard
Do not stop after a structural validator passes
Do not return the course map or a planning pack
They are internal generation state only

Deliver the first user visible course file only after `PASS`

The final spoken variety integrity pass must run after the last content rewrite because any later rewrite can reintroduce translated or written register speech
The final evergreen pass must run on the exact exported text because a late example can reintroduce a volatile price limit trend or interface path
Do not treat an earlier language or evergreen review as valid after the text changes

Ask the user only when a missing decision would materially change the product promise audience market instructor identity or authorised scope and cannot be resolved from supplied context

If research review rewriting or formatting can resolve the issue autonomously continue internally

First request final result does not mean accepting the first generated prose
It means completing the full internal production loop before returning one filming ready DOCX

## No hidden dependency rule

The course may welcome learners with and without prior exposure but must not pretend that advanced expertise has no prerequisites

Use just in time foundations
Define a concept before first required use
Give experienced learners a reason to stay by correcting weak mental models and exposing real tradeoffs
Do not repeatedly address groups as beginners or experienced people
Speak to the shared task

## Honest outcome rule

A course may create readiness for a junior paid task a portfolio piece a supervised role or a defined professional workflow
It may not promise mastery merely because all topics were mentioned
It may not claim work readiness when a learner would reach an unintroduced action decision access requirement client interaction correction or handoff in the middle of the real task

When available duration cannot support the requested outcome

- Narrow the promise
- Reduce the range of contexts
- Move optional specialisations outside the core
- Keep the full execution diagnosis and delivery loop for the narrower promise

Never preserve a grand promise by replacing skill with superficial coverage

## Real performance episode integrity

Every course must define the authentic episode in which the promised capability is used
The episode may be a workflow conversation call teaching session interpretation task decision case production sequence system operation physical action or another bounded real use pattern

Trace from the first authentic trigger or input through successful completion and likely recovery
Include counterpart signals environmental conditions variation time pressure documentation escalation and handoff only when the promise requires them

The internal object may retain the name `REAL_WORKFLOW_TRACE` for compatibility
In non business courses interpret workflow as the ordered real performance episode rather than forcing client or employment language

A course is incomplete when the learner reaches an unintroduced perception decision phrase response action check correction or completion step during the promised episode

## Real workflow integrity

For every job oriented or client facing promise trace the actual task from the first request condition or input through acceptance delivery handoff and likely recovery

Include the central craft and the work around it

- Clarifying the request and success condition
- Gathering inputs assets access permissions and missing information
- Setting scope time cost and responsibility boundaries when the role requires them
- Preparing the environment files data or workspace
- Executing the work
- Inspecting quality and diagnosing failure
- Handling feedback revisions changed constraints and approval
- Delivering packaging documenting and handing off
- Recovering escalating or communicating a limitation when the normal path breaks

Do not force irrelevant business stages into a narrow promise
Do not omit a required stage because it is less exciting than the central craft
Every required stage must be taught demonstrated practised or supported by an explicit pre existing learner asset before the learner needs it
A module project may provide visible practice proof but it is not a substitute for teaching the step

## Course sufficiency and duration rule

The planning anchor for a standard Rukn course is approximately 120 spoken minutes
This is an average rather than a quota minimum or ceiling

Derive total duration and reel count from

- Distinct capability changes
- Real performance episode stages
- Real workflow stages when the promise includes them
- Demonstration runtime
- Practice preparation and project explanation
- Required variation diagnosis correction and handoff
- The time needed to explain conditions boundaries and failure recovery

Every standalone teaching reel must run between 60 and 300 spoken seconds after visible demonstration pauses are included
No default reel count ceiling exists
Thirty reels and forty minutes are not sufficient merely because every topic title appears
A very large map including a count near 180 reels is acceptable when distinct capability changes and real performance episode or workflow coverage genuinely require it
That count is never a target

Do not lengthen a course to look substantial
Do not compress a course to look efficient
Use exactly the duration needed to fulfil the promise without repetition filler or missing competence

## Strict evergreen outcome

The course must not depend on fast expiring spoken facts

Do not place exact current prices fees subscriptions plan limits salaries rates rankings temporary trends current statistics fragile interface paths or similar volatile details in the recorded script

When a changing value matters

- Teach the stable variables and decision
- Teach the consequence of the value changing
- Tell the learner how to verify the live value at the moment of work
- Keep the actual current value internal and out of the DOCX

Do not add a date disclaimer and keep the volatile number
Do not move the volatile number into a companion file
Rewrite the teaching until ordinary market or platform change does not force rerecording

## Reuse without imitation

Reuse principles quality gates and reasoning structures
Do not reuse domain examples title formulas hooks closing formulas module bridge formulas paragraph skeletons or fixed counts from a reference course

Every new course must derive its own

- Learner transformation
- Capability graph
- Market pack
- Terminology
- Practice model
- Progression
- Module closure logic
- Voice calibration
- Lesson lengths
- Shareable insights

## Stop conditions

Stop before expensive full generation when any of these is unresolved

- The promise has no observable end performance
- The architecture is selected from the course title or one family label instead of capability level performance mapping
- A required capability has no accepted primary adapter native learning cycle or honest evidence form
- `PERFORMANCE_EPISODE_MAP` `CAPABILITY_ADAPTER_MAP` or `CAPABILITY_MODE_MATRIX` contains a material unresolved row
- An unfamiliar domain is being handled with generic explanation instead of a practitioner grounded custom adapter
- The audience has contradictory entry assumptions
- Required facts lack an acceptable authority source
- The current market or legal context materially changes the advice and has not been verified
- The requested duration cannot support the promised competence
- Projects cannot be performed with the learner access budget or tools
- A module project cannot produce a clear image screenshot or file that represents the intended capability
- A planned attachment requires hidden context an external link unseen process unsupported parsing before its decisive result can be inspected
- The last boundary project does not represent most core capabilities and every promise critical capability whose absence would invalidate readiness
- The final module introduces a new promise critical standalone capability that would need another formal upload to prove honestly
- A required real workflow stage remains unmapped or depends on an unavailable asset access permission client response or unexplained action
- The duration budget compresses a required capability or violates the one to five minute reel contract
- The instructor identity language or address form is unknown and would materially change the script
- The instructor would need to claim experience results or authority that has not been established
- The map contains unresolved duplicate outcomes
- A non final module has no honest closure condition or depends on an artificial teaser
- The promised course cannot avoid fast expiring spoken facts without narrowing or reframing its promise
- The final script would require a separately generated learner or platform companion file

Return the smallest decision request needed to continue only when the user must choose
Do not guess a product defining choice

## Acceptance mindset

Treat a plausible draft as untrusted
Require evidence that it is useful accurate natural applicable non repetitive evergreen and ready to film
Critique the meaning before the prose
Rewrite the smallest scope that fixes the cause
If the map caused the failure repair the map rather than repeatedly rewriting a lesson
If a volatile fact caused the failure replace the teaching logic rather than adding a warning
